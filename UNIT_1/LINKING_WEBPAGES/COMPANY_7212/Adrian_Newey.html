Adrian Newey

Adrian Martin Newey, OBE (born 26 December 1958) is a British Formula One engineer. He is the chief technical officer of the Red Bull Racing F1 team.
Newey has worked in both Formula One and IndyCar racing
as a race engineer, aerodynamicist, designer and technical director and enjoyed success in both categories. Newey is regarded by many to be one of the greatest engineers in Formula One history; his designs have won numerous titles and more than 200 Grands Prix. Newey is also one of the most successful designers, winning eleven Constructors' Championships with three different Formula One teams, and with seven different drivers winning the Drivers' Championship driving Newey's designs.
After designing championship-winning Formula One cars for Williams F1 and McLaren, Newey moved to Red Bull Racing in 2006, his cars winning the Formula One drivers' and
constructors' championships consecutively from 2010 to 2013, the drivers' championship in 2021 and both championships in 2022. Newey's designs also won the 1985 and 1986 CART titles.
