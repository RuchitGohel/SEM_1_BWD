About us

Red Bull Racing, currently competing as Oracle Red Bull Racing and also known simply as Red Bull or RBR,
is a Formula One racing team, racing under an Austrian licence and based in the United Kingdom.
It is one of two Formula One teams owned by conglomerate Red Bull GmbH, the other being Scuderia AlphaTauri.
The Red Bull Racing team has been managed by Christian Horner since its formation in 2005.
